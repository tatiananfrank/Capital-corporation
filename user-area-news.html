Everyday's profit gaining!

Dear Partners! Starting October 5th all portfolios profit is to be credited daily from Monday to Friday, at 18:00 HKT (UTC +8).

Profit for upraged portfolios and portfolios purchased from 05:00 to 18:00 is to be credited the next day.

Portfolios profit is not to be credited on Saturdays and Sundays.

Capital Corporation's top management expresses it's gratitude to all Agents for their active development and promotion.

Many interesting updates to make investments even more attainable are soon to come, stay tuned.

New goals - new opportunities!

Based on the results of the Partners Program audit we reached a decision to radically change the concept of calculating the comission income.

Starting September 8th the Agent's comission income is not to be charged from weekly portofolio income, but is to be charged from specially established Company's fund assigned to agents' payout.

Now, when an investment portfolio is bought in your stucture you'll get an instant comission payout depending on your career level:

Platinum Director 14 %

Platinum agent 10%

Beginner 3%

For our Partners and Investors we've prepared a step-by-step development plan including new marketing and investment vehicles.

We'll keep you updated in our News, stay tuned!
