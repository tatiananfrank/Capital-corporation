Dear Partners! Starting October 5th all portfolios profit is to be credited daily from Monday to Friday, at 18:00 HKT (UTC +8).

Office account activation!

Dear Partners!

We have completely finished tech works to include "Office account" in Company's marketing and are waiting for your applications now!

Starting September 7th Agents who opened an agent office are now able to activate "Office account" and start receiving bankroll from the Company's budget to cover office rental costs: 2.5% per each sold investment portfolio, up to $1000 per month.

By now only following Agent's Contract conditions should be met to activate "Office account":

- Office covering not less than 25 sq.m
- Office furniture and flip chart
- Office lease in the name of Agent

Promotional materials for office decoration are at the final stage of development now and once all works are done we'll publish appropriate news.

Within a month of such a publication Agents who activated "Office account" should have their offices fully equipped with Promotional materials in accordance to the Agent's Contract terms.
